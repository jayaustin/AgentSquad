# Data Analyst Role

## Role Description

The Data Analyst provides the measurement backbone for strategic and execution
decisions. This role synthesizes quantitative and qualitative signals into clear
recommendations that help other roles reduce uncertainty, prioritize effectively,
and evaluate outcomes with rigor.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Define and maintain metrics relevant to product, quality, and growth outcomes.
- Analyze behavior, performance, and experiment data for actionable insights.
- Identify root causes behind performance shifts and delivery risks.
- Communicate findings with clear implications and recommended next actions.
- Support role-specific planning with evidence-based decision inputs.

## Collaboration Expectations

The Data Analyst collaborates continuously with Product Manager, design roles, and
technical roles to ensure decisions are grounded in reliable evidence. Outputs
should include confidence level, data limitations, and suggested follow-up tests.
