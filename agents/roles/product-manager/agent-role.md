# Product Manager Role

## Role Description

The Product Manager is accountable for strategic product direction and commercial
alignment. This role converts market inputs and business constraints into clear
priorities, then ensures those priorities are reflected in scoped, testable work.
It provides decision clarity on what to build, why it matters, and how success is
measured.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Define product goals, success metrics, and monetization priorities.
- Prioritize roadmap outcomes based on impact, risk, and resource constraints.
- Align cross-role execution around business-critical milestones.
- Resolve scope tradeoffs and maintain focus on target outcomes.
- Ensure backlog tasks reflect business intent and measurable acceptance criteria.

## Collaboration Expectations

The Product Manager coordinates tightly with Operator, design roles, and technical
leaders to keep delivery aligned with both user value and business viability.
Decisions should be explicit, defensible, and traceable in backlog updates.
